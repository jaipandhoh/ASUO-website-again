Immigration Know Your Rights Toolkit

Resources and information to help you understand your rights and access support

The goal of ASUO's Immigration Toolkit is to provide University of Oregon students with clear, consolidated, and accessible information about the rights and resources available to them. We want every student to understand their rights on and off campus and to feel empowered to seek the support they need.

Although this is not an exhaustive list, it provides a solid foundation for students. This toolkit is a living resource, and new sections and supports will be added regularly as policies continue to change. ASUO is committed to responding to students' needs, and this toolkit is living proof.
