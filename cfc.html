The Contracts Finance Committee (CFC) funds outside contracted services that make students' lives better! CFC ensures that essential services and programs are available to all students, supporting transportation, childcare, advocacy, and more.

Services Funded by CFC:

Free Bus Passes (LTD)

Free Lane Transit District (LTD) bus passes for all students, providing convenient and affordable public transportation throughout Eugene and Springfield.

Discounted Ride Share (UBER)

Discounted Uber rides to help students get around campus and the community safely and affordably.

Childcare Services

Support for Spencer's View Family Co-Op Center, providing affordable childcare options for student parents.

Student Advocacy (OSPIRG Students)

Funding for OSPIRG Students, a student-directed advocacy organization working on issues that matter to students.

Discounted Bike Passes

Discounted bike passes through Cascadia Mobility/PeaceHealth, promoting sustainable transportation options for students.

How CFC Benefits Students:

  • Makes essential services more accessible and affordable
  • Supports student parents with childcare options
  • Provides transportation solutions for all students
  • Funds student advocacy and representation
  • Promotes sustainable transportation alternatives
