The Departments Finance Committee (DFC) allocates I-Fee funds to essential campus departments and services that directly support student life, engagement, and success at the University of Oregon.

Departments Funded by DFC:

Accessible Education Center

Supporting students with disabilities

Basic Needs Program

Supporting students' basic needs and resources

Committee For Musical Arts

Supporting musical arts and programs

Duck Rides

Safe transportation services for students

Forensics

Debate and speech programs

Holden Center

Leadership and community engagement programs

LGBTQ Services

Supporting LGBTQ+ students and education

Multicultural Center

Supporting diversity and inclusion on campus

Oregon Athletic Bands

Marching band and athletic music programs

Prison Education Program

Educational opportunities for incarcerated individuals

Student Conflict Resolution Center (SCRC)

Conflict resolution and mediation services

Student Government Engagement and Success

Training and development for student leaders

University Theatre

Theatre productions and programs

Women's Center

Supporting women and gender equity on campus

Zero Waste

Sustainability and waste reduction programs

What DFC Does:

  • Reviews and allocates funding for campus departments
  • Ensures student services are properly funded
  • Supports departments that enhance student experience
  • Maintains accountability for I-Fee allocations
