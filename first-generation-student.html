Resources for First Generation Students

Specialized resources and support for first-generation college students at the University of Oregon through ASUO.

üéì First Generation Student Representation

First-generation students have unique needs and perspectives that ASUO works to address through dedicated representation and services:

First Generation Senate Seats

  • Dedicated Representation: First-generation students have specific Senate seats representing their interests
  • Policy Advocacy: First-generation senators advocate for policies affecting first-generation students
  • Funding Priorities: Input on how I-Fee funds are allocated to first-generation student services
  • Academic Affairs: Representation on academic committees and policy discussions

First Generation Student Organizations

  • First Generation Student Union: Connect with the First Generation Student Union for additional advocacy
  • Peer Support Groups: ASUO supports first-generation student organizations and support groups
  • Mentorship Networks: Connect with other first-generation students and alumni

üí∞ Financial Support for First Generation Students

ASUO provides various financial resources specifically designed for first-generation students:

Academic Support Funding

  • Textbook Assistance: Funding for textbooks and course materials
  • Technology Support: Laptop and technology lending programs
  • Academic Supplies: Funding for academic supplies and equipment
  • Professional Development: Workshops, training, and professional development opportunities

Emergency and Support Funding

  • Emergency Grants: Financial assistance for unexpected expenses
  • Housing Assistance: Resources for housing-related financial challenges
  • Healthcare Support: Information about student health insurance options
  • Transportation Support: Assistance with transportation costs and resources

ASUO supports first-generation students' academic and professional growth through various programs:

Academic Support Services

  • Study Skills Workshops: Resources for developing effective study habits
  • Writing Centers: Access to writing support and editing services
  • Academic Advising: Specialized advising for first-generation students
  • Time Management: Workshops on balancing academic and personal responsibilities

Career Development

  • Career Exploration: Workshops on various career paths and opportunities
  • Networking Events: Opportunities to connect with professionals and alumni
  • Resume and Cover Letter Help: Assistance with job applications and professional documents
  • Interview Preparation: Mock interviews and interview skill development

üë• Community and Networking

Build connections and find community as a first-generation student through ASUO programs:

First Generation Student Events

  • First Generation Mixers: Social events specifically for first-generation students
  • Success Stories: Events featuring first-generation student and alumni success stories
  • Mentorship Programs: Connect with faculty, staff, and alumni who were first-generation students
  • Cultural Events: Celebrate diversity and cultural heritage within the first-generation community

Support Networks

  • Peer Support Groups: Connect with other first-generation students facing similar challenges
  • Mental Health Resources: Counseling and wellness services for first-generation students
  • Family Support: Resources for navigating family dynamics and expectations
  • Academic Confidence: Programs to build confidence and academic success

ASUO provides information and support for first-generation students and their families:

Family Resources

  • Family Orientation: Special orientation programs for first-generation families
  • Parent/Family Events: Events to help families understand college life
  • Communication Resources: Tools for maintaining family connections while at college
  • Cultural Bridge Programs: Programs that honor family culture while embracing college life

Community Building

  • First Generation Alumni Network: Connect with successful first-generation alumni
  • Community Partnerships: Partnerships with local organizations supporting first-generation students
  • Cultural Celebrations: Events that celebrate first-generation student achievements
