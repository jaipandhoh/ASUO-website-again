Law students have unique needs and perspectives that ASUO works to address through dedicated representation and services:

Law Student Senate Seats

  • Dedicated Representation: Law students have specific Senate seats representing their interests
  • Policy Advocacy: Law student senators advocate for policies affecting legal education
  • Funding Priorities: Input on how I-Fee funds are allocated to law student services
  • Academic Affairs: Representation on academic committees and policy discussions

Law Student Organizations

  • Student Bar Association: Connect with the Student Bar Association for additional advocacy
  • Specialized Groups: ASUO supports law student organizations within the law school
  • Professional Networks: Connect with law students across different specializations
