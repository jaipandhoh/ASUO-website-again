The Programs Finance Committee (PFC) is responsible for allocating I-Fee funds to over 200 student organizations and programs at the University of Oregon. PFC ensures that student groups have the resources they need to thrive and serve the campus community.

What PFC Funds:

  • Student organizations and clubs
  • Campus programs and events
  • Student-led initiatives
  • Organizational operating expenses
  • Program development and growth
